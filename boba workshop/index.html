HACK CLUB

Hack Club is a global community of high school students who are passionate about coding and technology. Founded by Zach Latta in 2014, the club offers a platform for young programmers to collaborate, learn, and create through hands-on projects and hackathons. The goal is to empower teens with the skills and resources they need to become future leaders in technology and innovation. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, Hack Club provides a supportive environment where you can explore your interests, build projects, and connect with like-minded peers from around the world.
